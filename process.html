How it was discussed

Debate and discussion

Nebraska's Legislature held committee hearings and floor debate on these proposals, with strong public testimony and extended debate. LB626 was debated early in the 2023 session and later, lawmakers debated combining abortion restrictions into LB574.

  • Committee hearings included public testimony.
  • Floor debate included motions, amendments, and attempts to end debate (cloture).
  • Some provisions from LB626 were amended into LB574 later in the session.

Sources: Nebraska Legislature transcripts list for LB626, and Nebraska Examiner coverage of the amendment and debate.

Passage or failure timeline

Visual timeline of key steps. Dates come from the Nebraska Legislature's official bill history pages.

Jan 17, 2023
LB626 introduced, referred to committee.
Feb 1, 2023
Committee hearing held for LB626 (listed on the official bill page).
Feb 28, 2023
LB626 placed on General File.
Apr 12, 2023
LB626 advanced from General File after a 33-16 vote to invoke cloture (end debate), then 33-16 to advance.
Apr 27, 2023
Cloture attempt on Select File shown as 32-15-2 on the recorded votes list for LB626.
Jun 1, 2023
Official bill history notes that provisions or portions of LB626 were amended into LB574 (AM1658).
May 19, 2023
LB574 passed on Final Reading with Emergency Clause (33-15-1), and was later approved by the Governor.
